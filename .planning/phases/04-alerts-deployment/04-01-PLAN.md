   - Uses st.secrets.get('smtp_email', os.getenv('SMTP_EMAIL')) pattern
   - Uses st.secrets.get('smtp_password', os.getenv('SMTP_PASSWORD'))
   - Uses st.secrets.get('alert_email', os.getenv('ALERT_EMAIL'))
   - Connects to smtp.gmail.com:587
   - Sends email with subject "VIX Sentinel Alert: HIGH Risk Detected"
   - Body includes: risk score, level, timestamp, ticker summary, sentiment
   - Returns True on success, False on failure
   - Catches all exceptions and logs errors
